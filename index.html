Our Services

Complete Landscaping Solutions

We offer comprehensive landscaping services to create, enhance, and maintain your outdoor spaces. From initial design to regular care, we've got you covered.

Garden Design

Professional landscape design that transforms your outdoor space. We create custom plans that blend aesthetics with functionality.

  • Custom 3D visualization
  • Material selection assistance
  • Sustainable design options

Planting & Softscaping

Expert plant selection and arrangement to bring life and color to your landscape. We specialize in plants that thrive in the Ethiopian climate.

  • Native & exotic plant options
  • Seasonal flower rotations
  • Drought-resistant landscaping

Irrigation Systems

Modern, efficient irrigation solutions that keep your landscape healthy while conserving water. Customized to your property's needs.

  • Drip & sprinkler systems
  • Automated timer controls
  • Water conservation solutions

Hardscaping

Create structure and function with our hardscaping services. We build patios, walkways, retaining walls, and more.

  • Custom patio construction
  • Natural stone installations
  • Decorative concrete options

Lawn Care & Maintenance

Keep your landscape looking its best with our professional maintenance services. Regular care ensures lasting beauty.

  • Regular mowing & edging
  • Fertilization programs
  • Seasonal cleanups

Tree Services

Complete tree care from planting to pruning. Our experts ensure the health and beauty of your trees while maintaining safety.

  • Strategic tree placement
  • Professional pruning
  • Health assessments
